About the Innovator

I’m Shashank Maurya (Ether), a Class 10 student at JNV Chandauli with a passion for science, innovation, and energy research. The Eco Power Sole is my vision to turn every step into a source of renewable electricity — blending wearable tech with sustainable design.

Project Overview

The Eco Power Sole converts walking motion into usable electricity using embedded piezoelectric sensors and hybrid microbial layers. Each step generates voltage that is stored in a rechargeable unit, capable of powering LEDs, sensors, or mobile devices. This innovation promotes self-sustained, portable power for daily use — a fusion of movement and green energy.

Working Principle

  • Pressure from footsteps deforms piezoelectric elements, generating electric charge.
  • The charge is rectified and stored in capacitors or mini lithium cells.
  • Stored energy can then be used via USB ports or wireless transmission.
